# Heal with Magic WhatsApp Follow-Up Templates

## New enquiry reply
`Hello, thank you for reaching out to Heal with Magic. I received your enquiry and I am happy to guide you. Please share a little about what you are currently facing, and I will help you with the best next step.`

## Free session confirmation
`Thank you for booking your free session. I have received your details. We can now continue here and I will guide you personally.`

## Program recommendation
`Based on what you shared, I feel the best fit may be the [program name] program. It is designed for [benefit]. If you want, I can explain how it works before you join.`

## Soft urgency
`If you feel ready, this is a good time to begin. Starting now can help you move through this with more clarity and support instead of carrying it alone.`

## Gentle follow-up after silence
`Hello, just checking in gently. If you still want healing support or want help choosing the right option, I am here for you.`

## Payment nudge
`If you would like to join, I can share the exact next step for payment and getting started today.`

## Reassurance for uncertain leads
`It is completely okay if you are still unsure. Many people first ask questions or take a free session before choosing a program.`
